Privacy Policy for Quick Status Saver

Effective Date: December 28, 2025

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

1. Introduction

TechSeeDrive ("we", "our", or "us"), the developer of Quick Status Saver, respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Our app collects minimal information necessary for functionality:

  • Media Files: Access to photos and videos from WhatsApp statuses stored on your device for the purpose of saving and managing them.
  • Device Storage: We access your device storage to save status files you choose to download.
  • Internet Connection: Used to display advertisements through Google AdMob.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To enable you to view and save WhatsApp statuses
  • To provide core app functionality including media browsing and saving
  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To improve app performance and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

All media files you save remain stored locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your personal media files to any external servers. Your saved statuses are stored in your device's local storage and are only accessible to you.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information including advertising ID, device information, and usage data. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

6. Permissions

Our app requires the following permissions:

  • Storage Access (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, READ_MEDIA_VIDEO): Required to access and save WhatsApp status media files
  • Internet Access: Required to load and display advertisements
  • Network State: Used to check internet connectivity for ad loading
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To send notifications about saved statuses (optional)

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except as required by law or as described in this privacy policy (such as data collected by AdMob for advertising purposes).

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data stored by our app on your device
  • Delete any saved media files from your device storage
  • Uninstall the app at any time, which will remove all app data
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
